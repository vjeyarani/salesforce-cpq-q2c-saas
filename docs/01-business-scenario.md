## Business Scenario

This project is based on a B2B SaaS company that sells a subscription-based
platform to enterprise customers.

### Products
- Platform Subscription (mandatory)
- Advanced Analytics (optional add-on)
- Premium Support (mandatory when Advanced Analytics is selected)

### Pricing Model
- Per user, per month pricing
- Volume-based discounts
- Approval required for high discounts

### Sales Process
Sales representatives create Opportunities in Sales Cloud and use Salesforce
CPQ to generate accurate quotes, apply pricing logic, and produce contracts.
